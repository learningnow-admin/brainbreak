We're going to play a game called Colour Touch! Here's how it works:

I'll call out a **body part** and a **colour** -- for example, "Elbow on something blue!"

Your job is to find something that colour in the room and gently put that body part on it. Walk, don't run!

Some ideas to call out:

- "Finger on something red!"
- "Knee on something green!"
- "Nose on something yellow!"
- "Foot on something brown!"

Once everyone has found their colour, call out a new body part and colour. To make it trickier, keep the old one in place while adding a new one -- just like Twister!
