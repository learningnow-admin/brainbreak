We're going to see how well we know the land around us!

Let's name as many **mountains in our area** as we can. If you can see one out the window, point to it!

Once we run out of mountains, let's keep going with other **land features** nearby:

- Hills and ranges
- Rivers and creeks
- Lakes and beaches
- Valleys and forests

Let's count how many we can name as a class. Does anyone know a story about how one of them got its name?
