We're going to play a game called Change Something! Here's how it works:

One person steps outside the room. While they're gone, we **change something**. It could be:

- Two students swap seats
- An object moves to a different spot
- Someone swaps jumpers
- A picture gets turned upside down

Then the person comes back in and has to spot **what has changed**. If they find it, the students who made the change take the next turn outside.
