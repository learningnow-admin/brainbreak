We're going to play a game called Huckle Buckle Beanstalk. I'll hide a small object somewhere in the room where you can see it without moving anything. Your job is to find it by walking quietly around the room. The catch is, when you spot it, you don't say anything or point at it. You just walk calmly back to your seat and say "Huckle Buckle Beanstalk." Keep a straight face so the others can't figure out where it is. The last person to find it hides the object in the next round.

Everyone put your heads down and close your eyes.

[Hide a small object somewhere visible in the room.]

OK, heads up! Start walking around quietly and see if you can find it. Remember, no pointing and no telling. When you've spotted it, make your way back to your seat and say "Huckle Buckle Beanstalk."

[Once everyone is seated, reveal where the object was hidden and start the next round.]
