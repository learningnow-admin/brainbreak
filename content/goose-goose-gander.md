We're going to play a game called Goose Goose Gander! Here's how it works:

Everyone sits in a big circle. One person is the **fox** and walks around the outside of the circle, gently tapping each person on the shoulder and saying **"Goose... goose... goose..."**

When the fox taps someone and says **"Gander!"**, that person jumps up and chases the fox around the circle. The fox tries to run all the way around and sit down in the empty spot before being tagged.

- If the fox makes it, the gander becomes the new fox.
- If the fox gets tagged, they have another go.

Make sure everyone gets a turn -- pick people who haven't been the gander yet!
