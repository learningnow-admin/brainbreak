We're going to play a game called Guess Who Is Missing! Here's how it works:

One person is the **detective**. The detective closes their eyes (or steps outside the door) while I quietly point to one student to go and **hide** somewhere out of sight.

Everyone else quickly **swaps seats** so it's harder to spot the empty place.

Then the detective opens their eyes and has to work out: **who is missing?**

If they guess correctly, the hidden student comes out and becomes the new detective. If they're stuck, we can give clues -- like the first letter of the missing person's name.
