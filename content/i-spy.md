We're going to play I Spy! Here's how it works:

One person picks something they can see in the room and says:

**"I spy with my little eye, something beginning with... B!"**

Everyone else takes turns guessing what it is. Whoever guesses correctly gets to be the spy next.

For younger students, use colours instead of letters: **"I spy with my little eye, something that is... green!"**
